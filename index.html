iSPACE competition

iSPACE Competitions provides a platform where students can be exposed to new challenging things that keeps them wanting to learn more.


In this platform, users can create and join various contests in different domains, such as coding, design, and more.


We offer different varieties of competitions, such as;

  • CODEFEST
  • DIGITAL LITERACY
  • ROBOTICS
  • VIRTUAL COMPETITIONS

At iSPACE Competition, we believe in creating a space where students from different schools, within schools, and even those participating virtually can come together to exhibit their skills and innovative spirit. Whether it's academic prowess, artistic flair, or technological innovation, we provide a stage for every student to shine.

Why Choose iSPACE COMPETITION?

The acronym iSPACE means Introduction to Systematic Programming, Animation, Computer Engineering and Electronics .

iSPACE COMPETITION is an academic program designed to train and encourage students in a career pathway and also develop innovative their creative abilities / skills that will be useful in areas problem solving.
